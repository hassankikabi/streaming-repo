ABOUT AL HASSAN ISLAM MEDIA

Our Story.

Thurayya Islam Media was founded in 2013 by a group of young Muslims who had a dream of using modern media channels to propagate Islamic knowledge and spread Dawah across the Islamic society in Uganda.

With a humble beginning of a small voice recorder and one camera we started recording sessions from different sheikhs and shared them via DVDs, CDs and audio memo via WhatsApp.

The idea of Thurayya Islam Media was then born, an Islamic media production organization with a vision of propagating reliable Islamic Knowledge (Dawah) from authentic sources using modern media channels.

At Thurayya we have been striving to improve the quality and outreach of our productions with a goal of ensuring that every Muslim with a phone gets a chance to partake of Islamic Knowledge no matter their schedule. Today with the onset of social Media; WhatsApp, Facebook, YouTube & Instagram the Thurayya Team has become a home brand reaching out to Millions of Muslims around the world.

For the last 5 years the Thurayya Team has also been able to organize Islamic seminars and Lectures plus engaging in presentations with International scholars in Uganda like Dr. Bilal Philips, Mufti Menk and ‘The journey of Faith Team’ among Others.
